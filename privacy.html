Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

OmniConvert ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OmniConvert mobile application (the "App"). We take the privacy of our users very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle any information you provide while using the App.

🔒 100% Local On-Device Processing: We do NOT collect, transmit, upload, store, or share any of your files, images, or documents. All conversions take place locally on your hardware.

1. Zero Data Collection & Sharing

Because the App executes completely inside a sandboxed client-side environment on your device:

  • We do not have servers to receive your files or documents.
  • We do not collect any personal identifying information (PII) such as names, emails, IP addresses, or location data.
  • No data, file metadata, or file content is shared with third parties.

2. Device Permissions Used

To enable offline utility features, the App may request access to the following hardware capabilities:

  • Camera Access: Used solely by the Camera to PDF tool to capture document frames. This video stream is processed entirely in your device's memory to generate a PDF and is never saved or shared.
  • Storage/Files Access: Used to write and save the generated PDF, image, or text outputs directly into your local Downloads folder.
  • Network/Internet Access: Used solely to load assets locally. No user analytics, conversion payloads, or file details are transmitted over the network.

3. Third-Party Services

The App is completely clean. It does not integrate any third-party SDKs, tracking pixels, cookies, analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics), or advertisement modules.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable and safe for users of all ages, including children, as we do not collect any personal information whatsoever.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published directly on this web page.
